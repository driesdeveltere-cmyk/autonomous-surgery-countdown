What counts

Autonomous = no active human supervision.
At scale = routine multi-center use.
Better = credible outcome data.

Common goalpost moves

“It’s autonomous” — but a surgeon is supervising.
“At scale” — but it’s 8 cases in one lab.
“Better” — but only surrogate metrics or edited demos.
